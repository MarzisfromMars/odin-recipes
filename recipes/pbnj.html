Peanut Butter and Jelly

A very quick and tasty meal!
pb&j and call it a day is my motto

Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly or jam of your choice
Steps
  1. Get the bread, jam, peanut butter, knife, and plate out on the counter
  2. Using the knife, get peanut butter and spread it on one slice of bread.
  3. Using the knife again grab the other piece of bread and put jam on it.
  4. Put the slices of bread together and there you go. You have a peanut butter and jelly sandwich!! Enjoy!!
